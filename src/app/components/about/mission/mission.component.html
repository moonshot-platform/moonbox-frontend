VISION & MISSION

Why did we start Moonshot?

We are a movement of like-minded, loosly connected individuals who believe in the transformative power of blockchain technology and the possibilities of the digital economy. We created the Moonshot project to provide a platform for NFT collectors and enthusiasts to create, trade, and explore the possibilities of NFTs.

Our decentralized marketplace is more than just a platform for buying and selling NFTs - it is a symbol of our commitment to empowering users through the use of blockchain technology.

We believe that everyone should have control over their digital assets and the ability to participate in the digital economy on their own terms.

But we are not content to rest on our laurels. We have recently fused with Ra8bits - an NFT gaming/collectibles project, expanding our ecosystem and opening up new opportunities for gamers and collectors alike to engage with NFTs.

We are driven by a set of core values that guide everything we do. We believe in innovation, constantly pushing the boundaries of what is possible in the world of crypto. We believe in transparency, ensuring that our users can trust us and our products. We believe in inclusivity, creating a community that is open and welcoming to everyone. And we believe in sustainability, ensuring that our products and services have a positive impact on the environment and society as a whole. Join us on this journey and be part of something truly special. Together, we can create a world where NFTs are more than just digital assets - they are symbols of our commitment to decentralization, innovation, and a better future for all.
