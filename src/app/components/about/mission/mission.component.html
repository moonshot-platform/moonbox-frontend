VISSION & MISSION

Why did we start Moonshot?

We at Moonshot want crypto to be more than just charts and candle chasing, we want to open up a world of entertainment and utility. Moonshot began as a token that rewards holders for sticking with the project; the longer you hold the more Moonshot you receive.

However, throughout 2021 and beyond, Moonshot will also be launching an NFT loot box programme, The Moonshooter animated NFT series, NFT gamification assets, arcade style games for competitive playing and a lottery.
