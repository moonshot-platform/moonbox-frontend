VISION & MISSION

Why did we start Moonshot?

We at Moonshot want crypto to be more than just charts and candle chasing, we want to open up a world of entertainment and utility. Moonshot began as a MEME token that rewards holders for sticking with the project; the longer you hold the more Moonshot you receive.

However, Moonshot is not just a MEME token anymore; Our flagship procucts are MoonBoxes, our NFT unboxing programme, and MoonSea - our NFT marketplace. Recently, we published the first prototype game of MoonArcade.Games.
