MOONSHOT MECHANICS

What
is Moonshot?

Moonshot is a deflationary, self-regenerating automatic liquidity providing protocol that pays out static rewards to holders. As our burn address is in the list of holders, the total supply of moonshot is forever decreasing.

Fee

Moonshot has a 10% fee on every transfer (both buy and sell). The fee break down is described below.



Reward distribution

Four percent of each transaction is distributed amongst Moonshot holders. This increases the incentive to hold the tokens in the wallets. As more transactions take place in the network, the Moonshot Balance of every individual increases as well.


Liquidity Adding

Three percent of each transaction is automatically added to the liquidity pool. The liquidity pool is a pool of BNB and Moonshot tokens locked in the smart contract that is used to facilitate trade between the two assets on a DEX. It uses the automated market maker provided by PancakeSwap. When tokens are sold, the amount of Moonshot in the pool increases and the amount of BNB decreases, causing the price to drop. Vice versa, when tokens are bought, the amount of Moonshot in the pool decreases and the amount of BNB increass, causing the price to rise. Since MSHOT v2, the LP tokens are owned by the smart contract and a mechanism was added that allows the project to withdraw at most 10% of the liquidity once every 4 weeks.


Project support fund

The remaining three percent is used to support the project's operational costs.
