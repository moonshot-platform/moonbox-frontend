Is Moonshot SAFU?

Our initial liquidity is locked by the team using the Dx Sale Network. You can view the lock here. The LP tokens generated by the protocol are owned by the smart contract. Liquidity removal is limited (10%) and time locked to a maximum of once every 4 weeks.

However, please keep in mind that the Liquidity Pool does not guarantee a specific price floor and that the LP tax we have intends to cushion large buys and large sells by adding part of the amount (3%) back into the LP.

Also, we have exempted wallet addresses from taxes (like, our project funding wallet, exchange's hot wallets and addresses for market makers). These addresses, do not pay a fee on transfer.
